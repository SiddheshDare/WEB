Note for Teachers and Administrators

The TCS iON Sharpner Learning Analysis Report is intended for teachers and school administrators. This report provides and aggregate view of the performance of students in a subject and can be used by school teachers and administrators for effective planning and monitoring of student learning. Teachers and school administrators can use this report to understand the performance of students at various levels of granularity, from overall subject level all the way to question level.

The indicators for each performance level, can be interpreted as follows:
